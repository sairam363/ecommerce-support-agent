# Shipping Policy

## Domestic Shipping (India)
- Standard shipping takes 4-7 business days across India.
- Express shipping (available in Tier 1 cities: Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Kolkata) takes 1-2 business days and costs an additional ₹99.
- Orders above ₹999 qualify for free standard shipping.
- We currently do not ship internationally.

## Order Processing Time
- Orders are processed within 24 hours of confirmation, excluding Sundays and public holidays.
- Orders placed after 6 PM IST are processed the next business day.

## Tracking
- A tracking link is sent via SMS and email once the order is dispatched.
- Tracking updates may take up to 24 hours to reflect after dispatch.

## Delayed or Lost Shipments
- If a shipment is delayed beyond 10 business days, customers should contact support for investigation.
- If a shipment is confirmed lost by the courier partner, a full refund or replacement will be issued within 5 business days.
