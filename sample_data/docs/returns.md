# Returns & Refunds Policy

## General Return Window
- Most products can be returned within 7 days of delivery, provided they are unused and in original packaging.
- Electronics and appliances have a shorter return window of 3 days due to manufacturer terms.
- Innerwear, cosmetics, and perishable goods are non-returnable for hygiene reasons.

## Conditions for Return
- Product must be unused, unwashed, and with original tags/packaging intact.
- Original invoice must be included or referenced.
- Products marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned.

## Refund Timeline
- Once a returned item is received and inspected, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Cash-on-delivery orders are refunded via bank transfer or store credit.
- Store credit refunds are processed instantly upon approval.

## Exchanges
- Size or color exchanges are allowed once per order, subject to stock availability.
- Exchanges follow the same eligibility window as returns.

## Damaged or Defective Items
- If an item arrives damaged or defective, customers must report it within 48 hours of delivery with photo evidence.
- Damaged/defective items are eligible for free return pickup and full refund or replacement, regardless of the standard return window.
