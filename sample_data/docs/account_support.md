# Account & Support FAQ

## Contacting Support
- Support is available via chat and WhatsApp from 9 AM to 9 PM IST, seven days a week.
- Email support responses may take up to 24 hours.

## Order Cancellation
- Orders can be cancelled free of charge as long as they have not been shipped.
- Once an order status changes to "Shipped," it cannot be cancelled, but can be returned after delivery per the returns policy.

## Account Issues
- If a customer cannot log in, they should use the "Forgot Password" option; OTP-based reset is available via registered mobile number.
- Multiple failed login attempts will temporarily lock an account for 30 minutes.

## Loyalty Program
- Customers earn 1 loyalty point for every ₹100 spent.
- Loyalty points can be redeemed at checkout, with 1 point equal to ₹1, subject to a maximum redemption of 20% of order value.
