# Payment & Pricing FAQ

## Accepted Payment Methods
- UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD) are accepted.
- COD is available for orders under ₹5000 only, and is not available in certain remote pin codes.

## COD Charges
- A COD handling fee of ₹40 applies to all cash-on-delivery orders.

## Discounts and Coupons
- Only one coupon code can be applied per order.
- Coupons cannot be combined with festive sale discounts unless explicitly stated.
- Referral credits can be combined with one active coupon.

## Price Adjustments
- We do not offer price adjustments for items purchased before a subsequent price drop or sale.

## GST and Invoicing
- All prices displayed are inclusive of GST.
- A GST invoice is available for download from the order details page for business purchases.
